# Smart Money Gig Marketplace - Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 What is Smart Money Gigs?

Smart Money Gigs is a location-based microwork marketplace where you can:
- **Find Opportunities**: Discover gigs and tasks in your area on a real map
- **Post Tasks**: Create gigs and hire others to complete them
- **Earn Money**: Get paid for completed tasks with secure escrow protection
- **Work Flexibly**: Choose when and where you work

## 🚀 Getting Started (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Open Smart Money
1. Go to http://localhost:3000
2. Login with your account (or create a new one)
3. Allow browser location permission when prompted

### Step 2: View Gigs on Map
1. Click the **"💼 Gigs"** button in the bottom navigation
2. You'll see a map with your location (blue marker)
3. The purple circle shows your search area (5km radius by default)
4. Purple markers show available gigs near you

### Step 3: Find Gigs
Use the filters on the right side:
- **Search**: Type keywords like "design" or "coding"
- **Category**: Filter by type of work
- **Budget**: Set minimum and maximum price
- **Radius**: Expand or shrink your search area
- **Search**: Click to find gigs matching your criteria

### Step 4: Apply for a Gig
1. Click on a gig card or marker
2. Review the details: budget, category, description
3. Click **"Apply Now"** to submit your application
4. Wait for the gig poster to hire you

### Step 5: Post Your Own Gig
1. Click the **"+ Post a Gig"** button (top right)
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: What needs to be done?
   - **Description**: Provide clear instructions
   - **Budget**: How much will you pay?
   - **Category**: Choose the work type
   - **Deadline**: When is it due? (optional)
3. Click **"Post Gig"**
4. Your gig appears on the map at your location!

## 💡 Tips for Success

### As a Gig Poster
- ✅ Write clear, detailed descriptions
- ✅ Set fair budgets based on work complexity
- ✅ Respond quickly to applicants
- ✅ Provide all necessary files/information upfront
- ❌ Don't change requirements after posting
- ❌ Don't hire without reading applications

### As a Worker
- ✅ Read the full gig description
- ✅ Ask questions before applying
- ✅ Deliver high-quality work
- ✅ Communicate updates regularly
- ❌ Don't apply for gigs you can't complete
- ❌ Don't ghost after being hired

## 🗺️ How the Map Works

### Map Controls
- **Zoom In**: Scroll up or pinch
- **Zoom Out**: Scroll down or pinch open
- **Pan**: Click and drag to move around
- **Click Markers**: View gig details

### What the Colors Mean
- 🔵 **Blue Marker**: Your current location
- 🟣 **Purple Marker**: Available gig
- ⭕ **Purple Circle**: Your search radius

### Search Radius
The circle shows how far you're willing to travel. To change:
1. Use the "Radius (km)" slider in filters
2. Minimum: 1 km
3. Maximum: 50 km

## 💰 Payment Process

### When You Post a Gig
1. **Budget Lock**: Your budget is held in escrow
2. **Worker Hired**: Budget is secured with the selected worker
3. **Work Complete**: Worker marks gig as complete
4. **You Review**: Check the work quality
5. **Payment Released**: Budget is transferred to worker

### When You Work a Gig
1. **Hired**: You're selected to complete the gig
2. **Work**: Complete the task as described
3. **Mark Done**: Submit your work
4. **Wait**: Gig poster verifies your work
5. **Get Paid**: Payment transferred to your account

## 🔔 Notifications

You'll get notified when:
- Someone applies for your gig
- You're hired for a gig
- A gig is complete and needs verification
- Payment is released

## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: Can I change my search location?
**A:** Yes! The app uses your device's GPS location. To change:
1. Move to a different location physically
2. The map will update automatically
3. Or manually grant location permission again

### Q: How much should I charge?
**A:** Consider:
- Time required: More time = higher price
- Skill level: Specialized skills cost more
- Market rate: Check similar gigs for reference
- Typical range: ZMW 100-5,000+ depending on work

### Q: Can I cancel a gig?
**A:** 
- As Poster: Yes, before hiring a worker
- As Worker: Only with poster's agreement

### Q: What if there's a dispute?
**A:** 
1. Try to resolve directly with the other person
2. Contact Smart Money support
3. We'll review the evidence
4. Support team makes final decision
